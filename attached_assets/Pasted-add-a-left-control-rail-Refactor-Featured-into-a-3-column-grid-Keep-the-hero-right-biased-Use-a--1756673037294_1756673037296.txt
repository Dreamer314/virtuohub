Wrapper around Featured row:
class="relative grid lg:grid-cols-[220px_minmax(0,1fr)_360px] gap-6"

Put the whole Featured card (media + text) in the middle column.

Remove any negative left margins on media.

Control rail (left column)

Vertical stack, centered:

Button style utility (reuse):
ctrl-btn = "h-12 w-12 rounded-full bg-white/6 backdrop-blur ring-1 ring-white/15 hover:bg-white/10 focus:outline-none focus:ring-2 focus:ring-white/40"

Middle column (your current Featured)

Keep your media at aspect-[16/9], object-cover, rounded, glow.

Keep the balanced, fluid headline classes so titles auto-fit:
text-balance text-pretty keep-words text-[clamp(2.25rem,6cqi,3.75rem)] leading-[1.05]

Keyboard + autoplay

Left/Right keys navigate. Space toggles pause.

Autoplay 5 s. Pause on hover or focus inside Featured.

Mobile

Hide the left rail (hidden lg:flex).

Acceptance

Drop-in JSX skeleton
<div className="relative grid lg:grid-cols-[220px_minmax(0,1fr)_360px] gap-6">
  {/* Left control rail */}
  <aside aria-label="Carousel controls"
         className="hidden lg:flex flex-col items-start justify-center gap-4 text-white/70">
    <p className="text-xs">Use ← → to navigate • Space to pause</p>
    <div className="flex flex-col gap-3">
      <button onClick={() => go(-1)} aria-label={`Previous slide (${i+1} of ${count})`}
              className="h-12 w-12 rounded-full bg-white/6 backdrop-blur ring-1 ring-white/15 hover:bg-white/10 focus:outline-none focus:ring-2 focus:ring-white/40">‹</button>
      <button onClick={() => go(1)} aria-label={`Next slide (${i+1} of ${count})`}
              className="h-12 w-12 rounded-full bg-white/6 backdrop-blur ring-1 ring-white/15 hover:bg-white/10 focus:outline-none focus:ring-2 focus:ring-white/40">›</button>
      <button onClick={() => setPaused(p => !p)}
              aria-pressed={paused}
              aria-label={paused ? "Resume autoplay" : "Pause autoplay"}
              className="h-12 w-12 rounded-full bg-white/6 backdrop-blur ring-1 ring-white/15 hover:bg-white/10 focus:outline-none focus:ring-2 focus:ring-white/40">
        {paused ? "▶" : "⏸"}
      </button>
    </div>
    <div className="flex gap-2 mt-2">
      {items.map((_, idx) => (
        <button key={idx} onClick={() => set(idx)}
                aria-label={`Go to slide ${idx+1}`}
                className={`h-2.5 w-2.5 rounded-full ${idx===i ? "bg-[#825FFF]" : "bg-white/25 hover:bg-white/40"}`} />
      ))}
    </div>
  </aside>

  {/* Middle: Featured card */}
  <section id="featuredV2" className="relative mx-auto max-w-[980px] px-6 md:px-0">
    <div className="grid md:grid-cols-[1.2fr_1fr] gap-14 items-center">
      {/* media */}
      <div className="relative">
        <div className="absolute inset-0 -z-10 bg-[radial-gradient(closest-side,rgba(120,100,255,.18),transparent_70%)]" />
        <div className="w-full aspect-[16/9] min-h-[360px] rounded-2xl overflow-hidden shadow-[0_0_90px_rgba(120,100,255,.16)] ring-1 ring-white/8">
          <img src={item.imageSrc} alt={item.imageAlt} className="w-full h-full object-cover object-center" />
        </div>
      </div>
      {/* text */}
      <div className="cq max-w-[38ch]">
        <div className="text-sm text-white/55">{formatDate(item.dateISO)}</div>
        <div className="mt-2 flex items-center gap-2">
          <span className={`rounded-full px-3 py-1 text-xs font-medium ${chipClass(item.tag)}`}>{item.tag}</span>
          {item.type ? <span className="text-white/55 text-xs">• {item.type}</span> : null}
        </div>
        <h2 className="mt-3 text-balance text-pretty keep-words text-[clamp(2.25rem,6cqi,3.75rem)] leading-[1.05]">
          {noWidow(item.title)}
        </h2>
        <p className="mt-5 text-lg text-white/80">{item.blurb}</p>
        <a href={item.ctaHref}
           className="mt-6 inline-flex items-center justify-center rounded-xl px-5 py-3 bg-[#6E4BFF] hover:bg-[#825FFF] text-white font-medium">
          {item.ctaLabel}
        </a>
      </div>
    </div>
    {/* keep mobile overlay arrows and dots here if needed */}
  </section>

  {/* Right rail spacer (your existing sidebar occupies this column) */}
  <div className="hidden lg:block" />
</div>

Why this is the best of both

Keeps your image + copy to the right like you prefer.

Fills the left void with useful controls and a small hint, so the area doesn’t feel empty.

Arrows are no longer on top of the image.

Keyboard and autoplay status are obvious. The toggle button uses aria-pressed for screen readers.
