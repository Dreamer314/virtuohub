What to change (plain English for your AI coder)

Increase image→text spacing

Desktop card grid: grid md:grid-cols-[1.1fr_1fr] gap-12 md:gap-16 xl:gap-24

Give the image frame right margin on desktop: md:mr-4 xl:mr-8

Constrain text width so headlines don’t crash into the image: max-w-[44ch]

Arrows layout and behavior

Arrows sit outside the image frame, vertically centered to the whole card.

Left arrow x-offset: -6 on md, -10 on xl. Right arrow x-offset: mirror.

Size: h-12 w-12 md:h-14 md:w-14 with ring on focus.

Use dark translucent background with subtle glow.

Disable while transitioning.

Add date line

Show a small muted date above the tag: text-sm text-white/50

Format: August 29, 2025 (use your locale formatter)

Fix categories

Spotlight

Industry News

Tech
