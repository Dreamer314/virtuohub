### 1\. The Cool, Industry-Specific Feature: "Quick Markup"

You are 100% correct that file attachments are essential. But to make it *special*, you need to solve the *next* problem: **visual feedback**.

The single most common "broken" workflow for creators is this:

1.  A modeler sends a `.jpg` of a new rig.
2.  The hirer (creator) sees a problem.
3.  The hirer must: download the image, open it in Photoshop/Paint, circle the problem, save it, and re-upload it.
4.  It's slow, clunky, and forces them to leave your platform.

Your unique, industry-specific feature should be an **in-chat "Quick Markup" tool.**

**How it works:**

1.  A user (Talent or Creator) uploads an image (`.jpg`, `.png`) to the chat.
2.  *Inside the chat window*, when you hover over the image, a new button appears: **"Review"** or **"Markup."**
3.  Clicking it opens a simple editor *inside the chat* (a modal).
4.  The editor has two tools:
      * **Pin Comment:** Click anywhere on the image to drop a "pin" and type a note (e.g., "This texture seam is visible" or "Move this vertex").
      * **Simple Brush:** A simple red brush to circle an area.
5.  The user hits "Send Review."
6.  This sends a *new* image into the chatâ€”the original, but now with the annotations and comments overlaid.

**Why this is a "Lifeboat" Feature:**

  * **It's Industry-Specific:** It's built for visual artists, 3D modelers, texture artists, and photographers.
  * **It's Sticky:** You are solving a complex problem *without* forcing them to flee to Discord or email. It makes your platform the professional tool of choice for *actually doing the work.*
  * **It Stands Out:** No other generic "job board" has this. It proves your platform is *designed* for the immersive creator economy.
